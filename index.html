The Game - Roguelike Asteroids

Despite not playing the original 1979 Atari game Asteroids until I started learning about game development in highschool, I became quite interested in making a game based on Asteroids, but with my own spin on it.

There are a handful of ideas I have contemplated since I thought about this game, but I have yet to have a proper opportunity to create it.
